Description             : [EN]
                          This map was created specifically for The Warp
                          project by Laocoön. It was conceived by me as an
                          alternative secret ending to the megawad.
                          
                          It is probably the largest and most elaborate map
                          in my career as a mapper at the moment. The
                          gameplay will be a non-stop action in the spirit of
                          Run&Gun with a very large-scale battle at the end
                          of the map. But there are also great opportunities
                          to explore the level due to the large amount of
                          secrets. The approximate time to fully complete a
                          level is from 40 minutes to 1 hour.
                          
                          This is beta 3 - only Ultra-Violence is available
                          to play! Other difficulty levels will be available
                          in the next updates.

Base                    : New from scratch
Build Time              : Six months
Editor(s) used          : Ultimate Doom Builder, SLADE 3, Aseprite, Audacity
                          
Known Bugs              : DSDA OpenGL+Mouselook completely break the map, you
                          should absolutely refrain from playing with such
                          settings.
                          
                          Doom Retro doesn't handle such large maps well.
                          UV-MAX in Doom Retro will be unattainable there as
                          some monsters don't teleport and get stuck, in one
                          secret the rendering breaks very badly.
                          
                          Mouselook can break some scenes, it's best not to
                          use it
                          
                          Large FOV in Woof!/Nugget Doom/Cherry Doom (and
                          possibly other ports) can also break some scenes,
                          it is not recommended to set values above 90
						  
						  GZDoom will be having performance issues
						  Also, the background on the screen with text is 
						  glitched in GZDoom
						  
May Not Run With        : Non MBF21 ports
                          
Tested With             : Nugget Doom v4.2.0, 
                          Cherry Doom v2.0.0,
                          Doom Retro v5.6, 
                          DSDA v0.28.3, 
                          Woof! v15.0.1
